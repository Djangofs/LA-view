Does this establishment use a private water supply?
What is an establishment?

An establishment is the location of your food business. If it is a mobile food business, please use the location where it is normally stored overnight.

It has a private water supply if it uses water that you take up from the ground by yourself. For example from a well.

I don't know if I have a private water supply

If you are not registered with a water supply company or paying a bill for water, this is an indication that your water supply could be private.
