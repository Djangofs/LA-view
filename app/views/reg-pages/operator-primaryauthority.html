What is Primary Authority?

Primary Authority is a legal partnership between a business or individual and a local council. It is a means for businesses with multiple outlets to recieve assured and tailored advice on environmental health, trading standards and fire safety regulations through a single point of contact. Read more about Primary Authority and how to get one.
